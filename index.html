What is SyncMy.Info?

SyncMy.Info is designed to help students organize their semesters.
Students can now sync deadlines for all their classes with just a few clicks. As an added functionality,
we have streamlined the syllabus-creation process for professors. They can
follow our intuitive system to quickly generate a syllabus.
